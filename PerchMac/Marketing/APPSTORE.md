## Tagline

## Description (Full)

**Your pocket field guide, always in the menu bar.**

PerchMac keeps your bird watching life at your fingertips — a lightweight menu bar companion that tracks your life list, surfaces recent sightings, and helps you remember every species you've ever spotted.

**What you get:**
- 🦜 **Life List at a Glance** — Total species count, recent additions, and your full life list right from the menu bar
- 📍 **Sighting History** — Every observation logged with date, location, and species details
- 🔍 **Species Explorer** — Rich species cards with taxonomy, conservation status, and field notes
- 🌲 **Expert Guide** — Seasonal insights, migration patterns, and birding event alerts
- 🤖 **AI Bird ID** — Identify birds from your observations with intelligent suggestions

Built for the nature-minded: warm, calm aesthetics inspired by vintage field guides and naturalist journals.
