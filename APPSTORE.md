## App Description

### Short Description (170 chars)

Perch passively tracks your travels, building a personal atlas of every city and country you visit. Start a trip, forget about it.

### Full Description

**Perch** is a passive travel tracker that quietly builds your personal travel atlas.

You don't log anything. You don't check in. You just start a trip before you leave, and end it when you get home. Perch does the rest — recording every city you visit, every country you cross, every kilometer you travel.

**How it works:**

→ **Start a trip** — tap once before you leave
→ **Live your trip** — close the app, travel normally
→ **End the trip** — Perch shows you everywhere you went

**What Perch records:**
- Every city you visit with arrival and departure dates
- Total distance traveled
- Estimated CO₂ footprint
- A map with all your stops pinned

**At the end of the year**, your travel summary: countries visited, cities explored, total distance, and your carbon footprint along the way.

**Privacy, always:**
Perch uses your location **only** during active trips. It runs on significant location changes — not continuous GPS — so battery impact is minimal. All data stays on your device. No accounts. No cloud. No data sharing. Ever.

**Upgrade to Wander ($4.99/mo)** for unlimited trips, 90-day history, and CO₂ tracking.

**Upgrade to Explorer ($9.99/mo)** for lifetime history, travel insights, map export, and multiple simultaneous trips.
